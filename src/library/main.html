Why I wanted to read these books?

By forcing yourself to read a book, you voluntarily become a slave to the book. When you finish reading a book, you feel like you are no longer a slave. As if you have just escaped prison.

By taking a loan, playing a game, listening to music, or even participating in society, you can also become a slave. Opening a bridge to something may turn you into a slave of that specific bridge/connection, and the truth is that almost everything in this world works this way. We sell our freedom either consciously or not.

But just like with the Babel, the escape(collapse) may also bring you profits in the end. The concept of cyclic enslaving and freeing yourself from something may be a good self-balancing lifestyle to live. To maximize the yield of Babel/Prison's heritage, I would suggest picking a prison with potentially worth potential.

Hence, I read these books.
