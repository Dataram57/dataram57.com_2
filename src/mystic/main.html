Mystic

Mystic is a pseudo-stealth game built on a Quake Engine, inspired by the Thief series and Quake mod "In the Shadows".

The current version, Enoch-0.9.0, features only 1 level, and not all story features have been implemented. Game development is in progress

Story

Durven was a man of unknown origin. In the kingdom of Bauland, he was known for his trade activities with Shadorian merchants. Selling guns to children and spreading antisocial heresies were the kinds of activities that eventually led him to be sentenced to life in prison. That was until the day a new prisoner accidentally dropped a mysterious amulet, from which the ghost of the Desertian Princess Deshira emerged and spoke to Durven.
